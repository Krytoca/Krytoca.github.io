About the band

Animals as Leaders is an American Instrumental Djent Metal band based out of Washington D.C.. Below will be a short description of the members of the band and a brief description of the Djent genre for which they are prolific in. There will also be a brief history of how the band was formed. Members

Currently Animals as Leaders only consists of 3 key members: Tosin Abasi who is the lead guitarist and bassist, Javier Reyes who plays a more rhythm style guitar and bass, and Matt Garstka who plays the drums. Their former members are Chebon Littlefield who played bass and keyboard, Matt Halpern who played the drums and Navene Koperweis who played the drums and the keyboard.

History

Animals as Leaders was formed by Tosin Abasi and contained him as well as the former 3 members. The band was formed after Tosin's previous band, a metalcore band by the name of Reflux, was disbanded. At first Tosin was asked to do a seolo album for the heavy metal record label Prosthetic Records. He declines as he thought it was too egotistical. After a year of studying music he took the label's offer and created Animals as Leaders, the name being inspred by a Daniel Quinn novel known as Ishmael. By 2008 all the initial band members would leave and 2009 saw Javier Reyes and Matt Garstka joining the band.

The Djent Scene

The specific name for the genre that Animals as Leaders plays is known as Djent. Djent is a form of progressive metal that focuses on onomatopoeic efffects such as high-gain, distortion, palm-muted, low-pitch sounds. The first band to popularize the technique is the Swedish band Meshuggah. Their guitarist Fredrik Thordendal was considered its originator and pioneer of the sound. A common visual feature is the use of seven-striong, eight-string, or nine-string guitars. An example would be Tosin Abasi using his preferred eigh-string guitar while touring or recording.
